## CHUNK 1 — Product Overview

### What credit cards does Axis Bank offer?

Axis Bank offers a wide portfolio of credit cards across multiple categories: lifestyle/travel premium cards, cashback cards, entry-level rewards cards, and co-branded partnership cards.

**Main categories:**
- **Premium / Travel Cards:** Magnus, Atlas, Privilege, Vistara
- **Lifestyle Cards:** SELECT, Signature, Reserve
- **Cashback Cards:** ACE, Flipkart Axis Bank
- **Entry-Level Rewards:** Rewards Credit Card, My Zone
- **Co-branded Cards:** Airtel Axis Bank, Flipkart Axis Bank, IOCL Axis Bank, Samsung Axis Bank

All Axis Bank credit cards participate in the **EDGE Rewards** loyalty program.

## CHUNK 2 — Magnus Credit Card

### What is the Axis Bank Magnus Credit Card?

The Magnus is Axis Bank's flagship **super-premium lifestyle and travel card** targeting high-income individuals.

- **Annual / Joining Fee:** ₹12,500 + GST
- **Fee Waiver:** Waived on annual spends of ₹25 lakh or more
- **Reward Rate:** 12 EDGE Reward Points per ₹200 spent
- **Accelerated Rewards:** 35 EDGE points per ₹200 on spends exceeding ₹1.5 lakh/month
- **Welcome Benefits:** Yatra flight voucher, Luxe gift voucher, PostCard Hotels voucher
- **Lounge Access:** Unlimited domestic + international airport lounge visits
- **Best For:** Frequent flyers, high-net-worth individuals who spend ₹3L+ per month

---

## CHUNK 3 — Atlas Credit Card

### What is the Axis Bank Atlas Credit Card?

The Atlas is a **premium travel-focused card** that earns EDGE Miles redeemable for flights and hotel bookings.

- **Annual / Joining Fee:** ₹5,000 + GST (no fee waiver, but milestone rewards cover the fee)
- **Milestone Reward:** 2,500 EDGE Miles (worth ₹5,000) on annual spends of ₹3 lakh
- **Reward Currency:** EDGE Miles (separate from EDGE Reward Points)
- **Lounge Access:** Complimentary domestic and international lounge access; quantum increases with tier
- **Tiers:** Silver → Gold → Platinum based on annual spend
- **Best For:** Frequent travellers who want to redeem rewards for flight/hotel bookings

---

## CHUNK 4 — ACE Credit Card

### What is the Axis Bank ACE Credit Card?

The ACE is Axis Bank's most popular **cashback credit card**, aimed at everyday spenders.

- **Annual / Joining Fee:** ₹499 + GST
- **Cashback Rate:**
  - 5% cashback on utility bill payments via Google Pay
  - 4% cashback on Swiggy, Zomato, Ola
  - 2% unlimited cashback on all other spends
- **Cashback Credit:** Directly credited to card account — no redemption hassle
- **Lounge Access:** 4 complimentary domestic lounge visits per year
- **Best For:** Salaried individuals who pay utility bills digitally and spend on food delivery

---

## CHUNK 5 — Flipkart Axis Bank Credit Card

### What is the Flipkart Axis Bank Credit Card?

A co-branded card with **Flipkart** offering enhanced benefits on Flipkart and partner brands.

- **Annual / Joining Fee:** ₹500 + GST
- **Cashback Rate:**
  - 5% cashback on Flipkart and Myntra
  - 4% cashback on preferred partners (Cleartrip, PVR, Uber, etc.)
  - 1.5% cashback on all other spends
- **Welcome Benefit:** ₹500 Flipkart voucher on first transaction
- **Lounge Access:** 4 complimentary domestic lounge visits per year
- **Best For:** Regular Flipkart/Myntra shoppers

## CHUNK 6 — SELECT Credit Card

### What is the Axis Bank SELECT Credit Card?

SELECT is a **premium lifestyle card** offering all-round benefits on travel, dining, and entertainment.

- **Annual / Joining Fee:** ₹3,000 + GST
- **Minimum Income Required:** ₹9 lakh per annum
- **Reward Rate:** EDGE Reward Points on every spend
- **Key Benefits:** Complimentary airport lounge access, dining privileges, golf programme access, concierge services
- **Best For:** Mid-to-high income earners wanting a well-rounded lifestyle card

## CHUNK 7 — Rewards Credit Card (Entry-Level)

### What is the Axis Bank Rewards Credit Card?

An **entry-level rewards card** for customers beginning their credit card journey.

- **Annual / Joining Fee:** ₹250 + GST
- **Reward Rate:** 2 EDGE Reward Points per ₹125 spent
- **Accelerated Rate:** 10X EDGE points on apparel and departmental store spends
- **Milestone Benefit:** 1,500 EDGE Reward Points on net monthly spends of ₹30,000
- **Lounge Access:** 2 complimentary domestic lounge visits per quarter
- **Best For:** First-time credit card users or those looking for simple rewards

## CHUNK 8 — Airtel Axis Bank Credit Card

### What is the Airtel Axis Bank Credit Card?

A co-branded card with **Airtel** offering cashback on Airtel services and other spends.

- **Annual / Joining Fee:** ₹500 + GST
- **Cashback Rate:**
  - 25% cashback on Airtel recharges, broadband, DTH via Airtel Thanks app
  - 10% cashback on Swiggy, BigBasket, Zomato
  - 1% cashback on all other spends
- **Best For:** Airtel subscribers who want to maximize savings on their Airtel bills
