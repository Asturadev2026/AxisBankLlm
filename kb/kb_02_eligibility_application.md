## CHUNK 1 — Basic Eligibility Criteria

### Who is eligible to apply for an Axis Bank Credit Card?

To be eligible for an Axis Bank Credit Card, you must meet the following basic criteria:

- **Age:** Between 18 and 70 years
- **Citizenship:** Indian resident or Non-Resident Indian (NRI)
- **Employment:** Salaried or self-employed
- **Income:** Minimum income requirement varies by card variant
- **Credit Score:** A CIBIL score of 750 or above is generally preferred
- **Existing relationship:** Existing Axis Bank account holders may get priority processing

> Axis Bank independently assesses credit risk per RBI guidelines — a high score does not guarantee approval.

## CHUNK 3 — Documents Required

### What documents are needed to apply for an Axis Bank Credit Card?

You need to submit the following documents:

**Identity Proof (any one):**
- Aadhaar Card
- PAN Card
- Passport
- Voter ID Card
- Driving Licence

**Address Proof (any one):**
- Aadhaar Card
- Passport
- Driving Licence
- Ration Card
- Utility bill (telephone/electricity — not older than 3 months)

**Income Proof:**
- Latest 3 months' salary slips (for salaried applicants)
- Form 16 / Income Tax Return (for salaried or self-employed)
- Audited P&L / Balance Sheet for business owners

**Photograph:**
- Recent passport-size colour photograph

**PAN Card:** Mandatory for all credit card applications (or Form 60 if PAN is not available).

## CHUNK 4 — How to Apply Online

### How can I apply for an Axis Bank Credit Card online?

**Step-by-step online application process:**

1. Visit [axisbank.com](https://www.axisbank.com) → Cards → Credit Cards
2. Browse and select a card that matches your eligibility
3. Click **"Apply Now"** on the chosen card page
4. Enter your mobile number and verify with the OTP sent
5. Fill in personal details: name, date of birth, PAN, address, employment type
6. Upload required documents (identity, address, income proof)
7. Submit the application and note the **Application Reference Number**
8. Track your application status via the website or Axis Mobile app

**Processing time:** Applications are typically reviewed within 7–10 working days. Instant approvals may be offered to pre-approved customers.

## CHUNK 5 — How to Apply Offline / at Branch

### Can I apply for an Axis Bank Credit Card at a branch?

Yes. You can apply at any Axis Bank branch.

**Offline process:**
1. Visit your nearest Axis Bank branch with all required documents
2. Ask the branch representative for a credit card application form
3. Fill in the form and submit with self-attested copies of documents
4. The bank will process the application and send updates via SMS/email

Axis Bank relationship managers may also reach out proactively to eligible account holders.

## CHUNK 6 — Application Status Tracking

### How do I track my Axis Bank Credit Card application status?

You can track your application status through multiple channels:

- **Online:** Visit axisbank.com → Credit Cards → Track Application → Enter your Application Reference Number and date of birth
- **Axis Mobile App:** Log in → Services → Track Application
- **Customer Care:** Call 1860-419-5555 (toll-free) or 1860-500-5555
- **SMS:** Send your application reference number to the bank's SMS service number
- **Branch:** Visit any Axis Bank branch with your reference number

## CHUNK 7 — What Happens After Approval

### What happens after my Axis Bank Credit Card is approved?

After approval:

1. You will receive an SMS and email confirmation of approval
2. The physical card is dispatched within **7–10 working days** to your registered address via courier
3. A separate PIN mailer may be sent or you can set your PIN digitally via Axis Mobile App
4. Upon card receipt, you must **activate the card** before use:
   - Via Axis Mobile App or Internet Banking
   - By calling the automated activation number on the carrier envelope
5. Your credit limit will be communicated via your welcome kit and SMS

## CHUNK 8 — Why Applications Get Rejected

### Why might my Axis Bank Credit Card application be rejected?

Common reasons for rejection include:

- **Low CIBIL score** (below 700): Indicates past payment defaults or high credit utilization
- **Insufficient income:** Salary below the card's minimum threshold
- **High existing debt:** Multiple active loans or credit cards with high outstanding
- **Employment instability:** Frequent job changes or self-employment without 2+ years of ITR
- **Incomplete documents:** Missing PAN, income proof, or identity/address proof
- **Previous defaults:** History of missed EMIs or credit card payments with any lender
- **Address in a negative location:** Axis Bank maintains an internal negative area list

> **Tip:** You can check your CIBIL score for free at cibil.com. Maintaining a score above 750 significantly improves approval chances.

## CHUNK 9 — Add-On / Supplementary Cards

### Can I get an add-on credit card for a family member?

Yes. Axis Bank offers **add-on (supplementary) cards** for family members.

- **Who qualifies:** Spouse, parents, siblings, or children above 18 years
- **How to apply:** Request via Axis Mobile App, Internet Banking, or by calling customer care
- **Limit:** The add-on card shares the primary cardholder's credit limit
- **Liability:** All spends on add-on cards are billed to the primary cardholder
- **Benefits:** Add-on cards participate in the same rewards programme as the primary card
