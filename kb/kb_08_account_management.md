# Axis Bank Credit Card — Account Management

> **Bot instruction:** Use this file to answer questions about managing an existing Axis Bank credit card account — PIN changes, credit limit management, card upgrades, address updates, international transactions, and card closure.

---

## CHUNK 1 — PIN Management

### How do I set or change my Axis Bank Credit Card PIN?

**Setting a new PIN:**
1. **Axis Mobile App:** Cards → Credit Card → Set PIN → Enter new 4-digit PIN → Confirm
2. **ATM:** Insert card → Select "PIN Change" → Enter old PIN (or generate new if it's the first time) → Set new PIN
3. **Customer Care:** Call 1860-419-5555 to request a new PIN via IVR

**Important PIN rules:**
- Your PIN should not be a predictable number (e.g., 1234, 0000, birth year)
- Never share your PIN with anyone — Axis Bank will NEVER ask for your PIN
- If you suspect your PIN is compromised, change it immediately

**Forgot PIN:**
- Use Axis Mobile App or Internet Banking to reset your PIN using OTP authentication

## CHUNK 2 — Credit Limit Management

### How can I increase or decrease my Axis Bank Credit Card credit limit?

**Credit Limit Increase:**
1. **Axis Mobile App:** Cards → Credit Card → Credit Limit → Request Increase
2. **Internet Banking:** Cards → Manage Card → Credit Limit Enhancement
3. **Customer Care:** Call 1860-419-5555

**Eligibility for increase:**
- Good payment history (no missed payments in last 6–12 months)
- Income increase since card issuance
- Low credit utilization on existing limit
- Improved CIBIL score

**Credit Limit Decrease (at customer request):**
- Right to reduce your own credit limit is protected under RBI guidelines
- Useful for controlling spending or reducing risk exposure

> **Important (RBI Rule):** Axis Bank cannot increase your credit limit without your **explicit consent**. If you receive an unsolicited increase, you can request reversal.

## CHUNK 3 — Card Upgrade or Downgrade

### Can I upgrade my Axis Bank Credit Card to a higher variant?

Yes, subject to eligibility.

**Upgrade Process:**
1. Contact Axis Bank customer care at 1860-419-5555
2. Or visit any Axis Bank branch
3. The bank will assess your eligibility based on income, credit history, and spending behaviour
4. If approved, a new card is issued — your account number remains the same

**Downgrade:**
- You may also request to downgrade to a lower-fee card variant
- Useful if you want to retain the account history but reduce fees

**Important notes:**
- Reward points balance typically carries over on upgrade
- EMI conversions in progress may be affected — check with customer care
- Annual fee changes take effect from the next billing cycle

## CHUNK 4 — International Transaction Management

### How do I enable or disable international transactions on my Axis Bank Credit Card?

By default, international transactions may be enabled on your card. You can control this via:

**Axis Mobile App:**
Cards → Credit Card → Card Controls → International Transactions → Toggle On/Off

**Controls available:**
- Enable/disable international usage (online + physical)
- Enable/disable contactless payments
- Enable/disable online transactions
- Set temporary/permanent transaction limits per channel

**Recommendation:** Keep international transactions disabled when not travelling abroad to prevent fraudulent international charges.

**Forex tip:** Inform Axis Bank before international travel to prevent your card being blocked for suspected unusual activity.

## CHUNK 5 — Virtual Credit Card

### Does Axis Bank offer a virtual credit card?

Yes. Axis Bank provides a **virtual credit card** for secure online transactions.

- **What it is:** A temporary card number with a limited validity period generated for a specific online transaction
- **Purpose:** Protects your actual card number from being exposed to merchants
- **How to generate:** Axis Mobile App → Cards → Virtual Card → Generate

> Virtual cards are particularly useful for first-time purchases on new or untrusted websites.

## CHUNK 6 — Changing Personal Details

### How do I update my address, mobile number, or email on my Axis Bank Credit Card?

**Mobile Number Update:**
- Must be done at an Axis Bank branch with ID proof (cannot be changed digitally for security reasons)

**Email Address Update:**
1. Axis Mobile App → Profile → Contact Details → Update Email
2. Internet Banking → Settings → Update Email
3. Customer care: 1860-419-5555

**Address Update:**
1. Axis Mobile App → Profile → Address → Update
2. Internet Banking
3. Branch visit with address proof (Aadhaar/utility bill)

**Important:** Keep your contact details updated to ensure you receive transaction alerts, OTPs, and statements without interruption.

## CHUNK 7 — EMI Conversion for Existing Purchases

### How do I convert an existing large purchase on my card to EMI?

If you have already made a large purchase and want to spread the cost:

**Post-purchase EMI conversion:**
1. **Axis Mobile App:** Cards → Transactions → Select Transaction → Convert to EMI → Choose Tenure
2. **Customer Care:** Call 1860-419-5555 within 30 days of the transaction

**Eligibility:**
- Transaction must be ≥ ₹2,500
- Not applicable for: fuel, gold/jewellery, cash advances, rent, wallet reloads
- Card should not be delinquent

**Processing fee:** May apply — check the specific offer terms at the time of conversion.

## CHUNK 8 — Card Closure Process

### How do I close/cancel my Axis Bank Credit Card?

**Pre-closure checklist:**
1. Clear all outstanding dues (full balance must be zero)
2. Redeem all pending EDGE Reward Points (they are forfeited on closure)
3. Cancel standing instructions/auto-debits linked to the card
4. Destroy the physical card after closure confirmation

**How to close:**
1. **Customer Care:** Call 1860-419-5555 and request closure → Receive reference number
2. **Written Request:** Send a letter to Axis Bank's Credit Card Division with your request
3. **Branch:** Visit any branch to submit a closure request in writing

**Timeline:** As per RBI guidelines, closure must be processed within **7 working days** of your request.

**Confirmation:** Request a written/email confirmation of closure for your records.

**No Objection Certificate (NOC):** After closure, request an NOC from Axis Bank confirming the account is fully settled.

## CHUNK 9 — Contactless Payments (Tap & Pay)

### How does contactless payment work on my Axis Bank Credit Card?

Axis Bank credit cards support **contactless payments** using NFC (Near Field Communication) technology.

- **How to use:** Tap your card on a contactless-enabled POS terminal (look for the contactless symbol)
- **Transaction limit:** ₹5,000 per contactless tap (PIN required for higher amounts, as per RBI guidelines)
- **Security:** Each contactless transaction generates a unique token — your actual card number is never transmitted
- **Enable/Disable:** Via Axis Mobile App → Cards → Card Controls → Contactless Payments

> If you prefer not to use contactless, you can disable it in the app and use chip+PIN for all transactions.
