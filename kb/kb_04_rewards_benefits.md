# Axis Bank Credit Card — Rewards & Benefits

> **Bot instruction:** Use this file to answer questions about earning rewards, EDGE points, cashback rates, lounge access, redemption options, and milestone benefits. Note the 2025 change on redemption fees.

## CHUNK 1 — What is the EDGE Rewards Programme?

### What is Axis Bank EDGE Rewards?

**Axis EDGE Rewards** is Axis Bank's unified loyalty programme that covers credit cards, debit cards, savings accounts, and other banking relationships.

- **Reward Currency:** EDGE Reward Points (for most cards) or EDGE Miles (for Atlas and travel-focused cards)
- **Earning:** Points are awarded per eligible transaction based on the card variant
- **Redemption:** Points can be redeemed via the EDGE Rewards portal (edgerewards.axisbank.com), Axis Mobile App, or customer care
- **Point Value:** 1 EDGE Reward Point = ₹0.20 (20 paise) for most redemption options
- **Point Validity:** 3 years from the date of earning
- **Minimum Balance to Redeem:** 300 EDGE Reward Points

## CHUNK 2 — How to Earn EDGE Reward Points

### How do I earn EDGE Reward Points on my Axis Bank credit card?

Reward points are earned on eligible transactions:

| Card | Base Earn Rate | Accelerated Earn Rate |
|---|---|---|
| Rewards Credit Card | 2 pts per ₹125 | 10X on apparel & dept. stores |
| ACE | Cashback (not points) | 5% on utility via GPay |
| Magnus | 12 pts per ₹200 | 35 pts/₹200 above ₹1.5L/month |
| Atlas | EDGE Miles (see CHUNK 6) | Tier-based bonus miles |
| SELECT | Points per ₹200 | Higher rate on select categories |

**Transactions that typically do NOT earn rewards:**
- Cash advances (ATM withdrawals)
- Fuel transactions (at most petrol pumps)
- Rent payments
- Wallet reloads
- Cash-equivalent transactions

## CHUNK 3 — Milestone Benefits

### What are milestone benefits on Axis Bank credit cards?

Milestone benefits are **bonus rewards credited when spending crosses a defined threshold** in a billing cycle or year.

**Rewards Credit Card:**
- 1,500 EDGE Reward Points on net spends of ₹30,000 per statement cycle
- Excluded transactions: rent, wallet reloads, fuel, cash withdrawals

**Magnus:**
- Accelerated rate of 35 EDGE points per ₹200 kicks in when monthly spend exceeds ₹1.5 lakh

**Atlas:**
- 2,500 EDGE Miles on annual spends of ₹3 lakh (offsets the ₹5,000 annual fee)
- Higher tier unlocks (Gold, Platinum) with higher milestone thresholds provide more lounge access

**SELECT:**
- Annual milestone benefits including bonus points or vouchers — refer to your welcome kit for the specific threshold

## CHUNK 4 — EDGE Miles (Travel Rewards)

### What are EDGE Miles and how are they different from EDGE Reward Points?

**EDGE Miles** are a specialized reward currency primarily associated with the **Atlas Credit Card** and used for travel-specific redemptions.

- **Earning:** Earned as EDGE Miles instead of EDGE Reward Points on Atlas card spends
- **Value:** Typically 1 EDGE Mile ≈ ₹1–2 depending on redemption partner
- **Redemption:** Can be redeemed for flight tickets, hotel bookings, or transferred to partner airline frequent flyer programmes

**Partner Airline Miles Conversion (Atlas card):**
- Air India Flying Returns
- InterMiles (Jet Airways partner programme)
- Singapore Airlines KrisFlyer
- Vistara Club Vistara (now merged with Air India)

> **Conversion fee (2025):** ₹199 per partner miles conversion transaction

## CHUNK 5 — How to Redeem EDGE Reward Points

### How can I redeem my Axis Bank EDGE Reward Points?

**Redemption channels:**
1. **EDGE Rewards Portal:** Visit edgerewards.axisbank.com → Log in → Browse catalogue → Redeem
2. **Axis Mobile App:** Go to Cards → Manage Rewards → Redeem Points
3. **Customer Care:** Call 1860-419-5555 and request redemption

**Redemption options:**
- **Product Catalogue:** Electronics, appliances, lifestyle products
- **E-Vouchers:** Amazon, Flipkart, Swiggy, Uber, etc.
- **Cashback:** Credited directly to your card outstanding
- **Airmiles:** Transfer to partner airline/hotel loyalty programmes
- **Hotel/Flight Bookings:** Book directly via EDGE portal using points
- **Partner Outlets:** At select merchant locations

**Redemption Fee (2025 update):**
- ₹99 per redemption transaction on the EDGE portal
- ₹199 for conversion to partner loyalty programmes

**Minimum points required:** 300 EDGE Reward Points

## CHUNK 6 — Cashback vs Reward Points

### What is the difference between cashback and reward points on Axis Bank cards?

| Feature | Cashback (ACE, Flipkart) | Reward Points (Rewards, Magnus, SELECT) |
|---|---|---|
| Form | Direct credit to card outstanding | Points credited to EDGE account |
| Redemption | Automatic (no action needed) | Must redeem via EDGE portal or app |
| Value | Exact ₹ value (5% = 5 paise per rupee) | Variable (1 point = ₹0.20 typically) |
| Redemption Fee | None | ₹99 per redemption (2025) |
| Flexibility | Lower — fixed to cash savings | Higher — products, travel, vouchers |
| Best for | Users who want simplicity | Users who want travel/product variety |

## CHUNK 9 — Point Expiry and Account Closure

### Do EDGE Reward Points expire? What happens if I close my card?

- **Expiry:** EDGE Reward Points are valid for **3 years** from the date of earning
- **Card Closure:** Upon closure of your credit card account, all accumulated EDGE Reward Points are **forfeited** unless redeemed before closure
- **Account Suspension:** Points may be frozen if your card account becomes delinquent (overdue payments)
- **Checking Balance:** View your current EDGE points balance via Axis Mobile App → Cards → View Rewards, or via the EDGE portal

> **Recommendation:** Redeem points before initiating card closure to avoid loss of accumulated rewards.
